Directive 01

Welcome, Guide

This class is about heritage, destiny, and the future.

Your Pre-Intermediate (A2/B1) student will explore the deep connection with nature and the courage to leave their comfort zone.

Grammar Targets:

  • 🐚 Gerunds as Subjects: (Ex: "Navigating is our history").
  • ✨ First Conditional: (Ex: "If I find the heart, I will save my people").

The Wayfinder's Kit

Oceanic Vocabulary

Click the symbols on the Tapa cloth to learn the nouns.

Ancestor

A person in your family from the past.

"Our ancestors were voyagers."

🗣️ Discussion Prompt:

Reef

A line of rocks or coral in the ocean.

"No one goes beyond the reef."

🗣️ Discussion Prompt:

What is a "reef" or barrier holding you back in your life right now?

Horizon

The line where the earth/sea meets the sky.

"See the line where the sky meets the sea?"

🗣️ Discussion Prompt:

What goals are currently on your horizon?

Voyage

A long journey involving travel by sea.

"We are preparing for a long voyage."

🗣️ Discussion Prompt:

If you could take a one-year voyage anywhere, where would you go?

Grammar Focus

Gerunds as Subjects

When we want to talk about an action as a concept or duty, we add -ING to the verb and put it at the beginning of the sentence.

"Navigating is in our blood."

"Protecting the island is my duty."

Grammar Module

First Conditional

We use the First Conditional to talk about future possibilities and real plans.

If + Present Simple, WILL + Verb

"If I find Maui, I will save my people."

Interactive Challenge

The Prophecy

Click the cards to match the "If" clause with the correct future result.

1. If Moana finds Maui...

> ...he will help her.

🗣️ Prompt:

If you needed help on a difficult journey, who is the first person you would ask?

2. If she doesn't cross the reef...

> ...the island will die.

🗣️ Prompt:

If you never leave your comfort zone, what will happen to your personal growth?

3. If they restore the heart...

> ...Te Fiti will heal the ocean.

🗣️ Prompt:
